Desserts

Tiramisu

Indulge in the timeless allure of our Tiramisu. Layers of delicate ladyfingers are generously soaked in rich, aromatic espresso and layered with a luscious mascarpone cream. As you savor each heavenly bite, the flavors harmonize, creating a symphony of coffee-infused elegance and sweet, velvety decadence. Topped with a dusting of cocoa powder, it's a dessert that transcends generations, an Italian masterpiece that leaves a lingering taste of amore.

Panna Cotta

Experience pure indulgence with our Panna Cotta. This Italian dessert is the epitome of silky elegance, featuring a delicate, creamy custard that gently melts in your mouth. Infused with the fragrant essence of vanilla, it's a sweet symphony of simplicity and sophistication. Topped with a luscious berry compote that adds a burst of fruity brightness, each spoonful is a journey through the finest flavors of Italy.

Affogato

Elevate your dessert experience with our Affogato. A scoop of premium, velvety vanilla gelato or ice cream is gently drowned in a bold shot of freshly brewed espresso. As the rich coffee meets the creamy ice cream, a delightful fusion of hot and cold unfolds before your senses. It's a moment of pure indulgence, where the bitterness of coffee harmonizes with the sweetness of the ice cream.Savor the perfection of Affogato, where dessert meets the art of coffee.

Cannoli

Discover the sweet embrace of Sicily with our Cannoli. These iconic Italian pastries are a masterpiece of indulgence. Delicate, crispy pastry shells, filled with a velvety blend of ricotta cheese, chocolate chips, and a hint of citrus zest. Each bite is a harmonious blend of textures and flavors, a joyful celebration of sweet, creamy richness. Topped with a dusting of powdered sugar, it's the quintessential Italian treat that promises a moment of pure delight.
