Starters

Gnocchi alla Sorrentina

Indulge in the comforting embrace of Gnocchi alla Sorrentina. Soft potato dumplings are lovingly enveloped in a rich tomato sauce, their pillowy texture complemented by the creamy allure of fresh mozzarella. Baked to perfection, each bite is a comforting journey through the heart of Italy, where simplicity meets the divine.

Arancini

Savor the allure of Sicily with our Arancini. These golden, crispy rice balls are a delightful blend of creamy risotto, mozzarella cheese, and a heartwarming hint of saffron. Each bite is a journey through the flavors of Italy, a perfect harmony of textures and tastes that will transport your taste buds to the Mediterranean coast.

Risotto ai Frutti di Mare

Delight in the ocean's finest with our Risotto ai Frutti di Mare. Creamy Arborio rice harmoniously combines with succulent shrimp, tender calamari, and briny mussels, all bathed in a rich, savory broth. A touch of aromatic herbs enhances this coastal Italian masterpiece, offering a taste of the Mediterranean's culinary treasures.

Bruschetta al Pomodoro

Savor the taste of Italy with our Bruschetta al Pomodoro. Delicate slices of toasted rustic bread, infused with the warmth of freshly rubbed garlic, provide the canvas for this Italian masterpiece. Topped with the ripest tomatoes, fragrant basil, and a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil, each bite is a journey through the sun-soaked fields of Tuscany.
