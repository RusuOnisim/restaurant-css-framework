Welcome to Quattro for Magic Italian Restaurant - Dilbeek & Bruxelles

Nestled in the heart of cityville, our Quattro for Magic Italian Restaurant has been a beloved culinary destination for over a decade. Founded with a vision to bring the authentic taste of Italy to our beloved community, we've been serving up the magic of Italian cuisine for nearly ten wonderful years. Our story began as a labor of love, driven by our passion for the rich flavors, traditional recipes, and warm hospitality that Italy is known for. Over the years, we've strived to capture the essence of Italy in every dish we create and every guest we serve. From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a cherished franchise, expanding our reach to both Dilbeek and Bruxelles. Our commitment to providing an enchanting escape into the world of Italian culinary excellence remains stronger than ever. Whether you're seeking the comfort of a classic pasta dish, the sizzle of a wood-fired pizza, or the sweetness of a delectable dessert like Tiramisu or Cannoli, we're here to delight your senses and transport you straight to the charming streets of Italy. At Quattro for Magic Italian Restaurant, we believe that great food has the power to create lasting memories. We invite you to join us on this culinary journey and experience the magic of Italy right here in your own neighborhood. Thank you for allowing us to be a part of your lives for the past decade, and we look forward to continuing to serve you for many more to come.
